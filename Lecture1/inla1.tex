\textbf{The short answer:}\\
\strut \\

\begin{quote}
INLA is a fast method to do Bayesian inference with latent Gaussian
models and \texttt{INLA} is an \texttt{R}-package that implements this
method with a flexible and simple interface.
\end{quote}
